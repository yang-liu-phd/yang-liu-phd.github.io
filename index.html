About me

I am currently living in Fairfield County, Connecticut. I was living and studying in Lubbock, Texas, and received my Ph.D. degree in Human Development and Family Studies from the Department of Human Development and Family Studies, Texas Tech University.

Outside of work, I enjoy telling stories with my children, cooking, and exercising.
